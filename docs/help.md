Aeon — how to use me over Telegram

• Type / to see every skill you have enabled, then tap one to run it.
• /skillname runs a skill directly (e.g. /digest). Add text after it to
  pass an argument, e.g. /article quantum computing.
• Just message me in plain English and I'll figure out what you want.
• /settings lists the skills currently enabled.
• /start shows a short intro (and can carry a deep-link payload).

Notifications may include buttons — tap Snooze or Mute to quiet an alert,
Re-run to run a skill again, or a link button to open something on the web.

If I ask you a question with a reply box, just reply to that message and your
answer goes straight back to the right skill.

Full guide: docs/telegram-commands.md in the repo.
